About Greenbush Lawn Service

POLO Lawn Services, founded in 2000, has always felt committed to keeping your lawn looking its best throughout the year by providing its customers with the best lawn care available. Active in the community, our managers and employees strive to keep up-to-date with all the latest technological innovations as well as staying current on invasive pests and turf disease trends and treatments.

We are the ultimate company for keeping your garden safe and tidy-looking!
